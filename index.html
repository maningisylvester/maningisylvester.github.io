FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

How do I know this will work for my hotel?
Start with a Digital Audit (KES 18,000). You'll get a full diagnosis of what's working, what isn't, and a clear action plan — before committing to a monthly retainer. It removes the risk.
We already use Booking.com and Expedia. Do we still need this?
Especially then. OTAs take 15–25% per booking. Every direct booking you generate saves you that commission. The goal is to reduce OTA dependence, not replace it overnight.
How long before I see results?
Paid ads (Google and Meta) show results within 2–4 weeks once campaigns are optimised. SEO and content take 2–3 months to build momentum. We're transparent about timelines from day one.
Do you require a long-term contract?
No long-term lock-in. Monthly retainers require 30 days notice to cancel. We believe the results should be the reason you stay, not a contract.
What does the ad spend cover? Is it included in the package fee?
The package fee covers strategy, management and reporting. Ad spend (Google and Meta budget) is paid directly by you to the platforms — we recommend a budget based on your goals and advise you throughout.
Do you work with hotels outside Nairobi?
Yes. Digital marketing works remotely. We work with properties across Kenya and are expanding across East Africa — Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda. If you have a hotel, we can help.
